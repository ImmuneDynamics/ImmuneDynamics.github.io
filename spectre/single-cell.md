## Overview

---

Though Spectre was not originally designed to work with genomic data, a number of Spectre's processing, analysis, or plotting functions can be helpful in analysing scRNAseq data. In Spectre v2, we have significantly expanded functionality for the analysis of data generated by any form of single-cell sequencing, cytometry, and imaging/spatial technologies. These updated workflows will be coming soon, but in the meantime, we have provided some helpful workflows below.
