Welcome to the research site of Dr. Thomas Ashhurst (Sydney Cytometry Core Research Facility, The University of Sydney) and the multi-disciplinary Immune Dynamics team. Our research involves the development and application of a range of high-dimensional single-cell cytometry/imaging assays, as well as computational analysis approaches, to map dynamic immune responses over time, space, and disease.
