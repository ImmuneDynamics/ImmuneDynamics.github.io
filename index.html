Home

Welcome to the research site of Dr. Thomas Ashhurst (Sydney Cytometry Core Research Facility, The University of Sydney) and the Immune Dynamics team, a multi-disciplinary collaboration of multiple research labs, combining expertise in immunology, infectious disease, high-dimensional single-cell & imaging technologies, computational biology, and data science. This team draws from the Sydney Cytometry Core Research Facility], the Discipline of Pathology, the Discipline of Infectious Disease and Immunology, and the School of Computer Science], at the University of Sydney]. Collectiely, our work involves the development and application of a range of high-dimensional single-cell cytometry/imaging assays, as well as computational analysis approaches, to map dynamic immune responses over time, space, and disease in a range of disease setting.
