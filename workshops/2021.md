# High-dimensional analysis homeshow 2021

**Monday 12-July to Friday 16-July**

<br />

## Overview

![Analysis](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tomashhurst/tomashhurst.github.io/master/images/Clusters%20wide.png)

We are pleased to announce we will be running the **High-Dimensional Analysis Homeshow** again in 2021, building on the success of the [homeshow in 2020](). Run in partnership with XXX, XXX, and XXX, we will be providing daily lectures and hands-on workshops over the course of a week, covering various aspects of high-dimensional analysis of cytometry and single-cell data. 

<br />

## Details

---

**When**: Monday 12-July to Friday 16-July 2021, lectures and workshops each day.

**Where**: Online (Zoom)

**Daily format**: Lecture & presentations (1000 - 1130 each morning) and hands-on workshop (1400 - 1600 hrs each afternoon)

**Daily topics**:

- Monday: overview of HD cytometry and single cell technologies and analysis
- Tuesday: clustering and dimensionality reduction analysis
- Wednesday: batch effects and batch alignment
- Thursday: differential, quantitative, and statistical analysis
- Friday: advanced topics (pseudotime, time-series, multi-technology integration, etc)

**Cost**: 

- Lectures/presentations: free
- Hands-on workshop (watching only): free
- Hands-on workshop (interactive attendance): $XX per person ($XX for students). Max 30 places for interactive attendance.
